Greetings

Start your conversations on the right foot with these common greetings:

  • Hello!
  • Hi there!
  • Good morning!
  • Good afternoon!
  • Good evening!
  • Hey!
Requests

Politely ask for what you need with these phrases:

  • Could you please...?
  • Would you mind...?
  • Can you help me with...?
  • Is it possible to...?
  • May I have...?
  • Would it be alright if...?
Expressing Gratitude

Show appreciation with these thankful expressions:

  • Thank you!
  • Thanks a lot!
  • I really appreciate it!
  • That's very kind of you!
  • You're a lifesaver!
  • I'm so grateful for your help!
Asking for Help

Don't hesitate to seek assistance using these phrases:

  • Could you help me, please?
  • I need some assistance...
  • Can you lend me a hand?
  • Would you be willing to help me out?
  • I'm struggling with...
  • Could you give me some advice?
Closing

End your conversations on a positive note with these closing remarks:

  • It was nice talking to you!
  • Hope to see you again soon!
  • Take care!
  • Have a great day!
  • Goodbye!
  • See you later!
