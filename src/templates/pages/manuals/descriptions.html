Physical Description

Describing someone's physical appearance can help in identifying them or painting a picture in someone's mind. Here are some common phrases for physical descriptions:

  • He is tall and slim with blue eyes.
  • She has curly brown hair and freckles.
  • They have a broad build and wear glasses.
  • He has a beard and wears a baseball cap.
  • She has a tattoo on her arm and piercings in her ears.
Personality Traits

Describing someone's personality can give insight into their character and behavior. Here are some phrases for personality traits:

  • He is friendly and outgoing.
  • She is shy but kind-hearted.
  • They are confident and assertive.
  • He is funny and easygoing.
  • She is ambitious and hardworking.
Clothing

Describing someone's clothing can add detail to their appearance. Here are some phrases for describing clothing:

  • He is wearing a blue shirt and jeans.
  • She is dressed in a formal black dress.
  • They are sporting a casual t-shirt and shorts.
  • He is dressed in a suit and tie.
  • She is wearing a hoodie and sweatpants.
