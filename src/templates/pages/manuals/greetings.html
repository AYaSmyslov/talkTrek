Greetings

Greetings are essential in social interactions and vary across cultures. They set the tone for a conversation and establish rapport. Here are some common greetings:

  • Hello!
  • Hi there!
  • Good morning!
  • Good afternoon!
  • Good evening!
  • Hey!
Introductions

Introductions help people get to know each other and initiate further conversation. Here are some phrases for introductions:

  • My name is [Name].
  • I'm [Name].
  • Nice to meet you!
  • It's a pleasure to meet you!
  • Allow me to introduce myself...
  • May I introduce myself? I'm [Name].
Closing

Closing a conversation politely is as important as starting it. Here are some common closing phrases:

  • It was nice talking to you!
  • Hope to see you again soon!
  • Take care!
  • Have a great day!
  • Goodbye!
  • See you later!
