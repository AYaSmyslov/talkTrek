Shopping

Navigate the world of shopping with these useful phrases:

  • How much does this cost?
  • Can I pay with a credit card?
  • Do you have this in a different size/color?
  • Where is the nearest supermarket/market?
  • May I have a receipt, please?
  • Is there a discount for buying in bulk?
Ordering Food

Enjoy dining out or ordering in with these common phrases:

  • Could I see the menu, please?
  • I'd like to order...
  • Can I have the bill, please?
  • Is there a vegetarian/vegan option?
  • How spicy is this dish?
  • Do you have any recommendations?
